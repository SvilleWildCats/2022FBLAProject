About Us

Emily Karr

Hello everyone! My name is Emily Karr and I am the owner of Photo's by Em. This venture started as an accident as I just really liked taking pictures of nature. My dad bought me a pretty nice camera to encourage me to learn more and practice as much as possible. As you can see I'm really into all things nature, but I am looking to branch out into doing portraits for people and eventually maybe I will get a chance to do weddings. This is all very exciting for me, if you can't tell, I hope you enjoy the site and the pictures I've taken so far.
If you have any questions or comments you can find my information on the contact page! All feedback is welcome!
